About

I'm a graduate software developer working in London and Kent. Some of the technologies I work with include Java, Python, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, HTML and CSS.

I've recently achieved an MSc in Computer Science at the University of Kent, earning a distinction. My thesis involved building a convolutional neural network with Python and Tensorflow, to classify images of honey bees.

My recent work experience includes web-development for local organisations along with developing my own projects. Before moving into software development I worked in research grant management for the University of Oxford.

In my spare time I enjoy outdoor activities, playing the guitar, and amateur photography.
